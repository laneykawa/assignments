New Future House Music

House music... When someone says hey, "I love music", I now think... hmmm well what kind. There are so many sub-genres of house that are all spectacular but yet have such differences in sound. One of the newer and extremely popoular sub-genre's is "Future House". Now let's define future house according to those who are more critial to the technial aspects; future house has characteristics of a muted melod ywith an elastic sounding drop. This genre was pioneered by Oliver Helden sand popularized with Tchami. Future house is a form of deep house.

Future of Production

Modern technology has advanced how people digest information, including music. This constant evolution of social media and production software advancements have lead us to a new era of music. This never ending change has changed how we create and market music. There is no longer as tight of controls from the big music labels as there once was. The power of the internet has connected billions of people and also created new exposure for upcoming producers and artists.
